Cleverly mobile thanks to mobility

Shopping with a station wagon, weekend trip with a convertible, moving with a van? A fleet of 2,930 cars is waiting for you at 1,500 locations throughout Switzerland - around the clock and in self-service. There are 200 e-scooters available to you in the city of Zurich in the free-floating system. With Mobility, you can be mobile at low cost at all times, but you avoid the obligations of having your own car. Whether insurance, cleaning or repairs: you no longer have to think about anything. A Mobility car is available from CHF 2 per hour and CHF 0.55 per kilometer.

Faster to the driving test

The more driving experience you collect as a learner driver, the safer you are on the road and the better prepared you are for the driving test. The learning driver offer from Mobility is the ideal addition to the driving lessons with us, for example if there is no car or only one machine available privately. Various vehicle models (manual and automatic) are available for private learning trips . The student driver subscription is available for CHF 49 and is valid for 6 months.

So that you can continue to stay cleverly mobile, we will give you a mobility test subscription (value CHF 43) after passing the exam .

We wish you good luck!
