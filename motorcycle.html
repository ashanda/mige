Motorcycle

Motorcycle course for the region of Aarau, Baden and Zurich

The Star driving school has established itself in the basic motorcycle course area for the regions of Aarau, Baden and Zurich and is the point of contact for an entertaining and interesting course. The courses are structured close to the exam and structured in an instructive manner. With a lot of fun and humor, the hours fly by.

Many underestimate the very high level that is required for the exam in Switzerland. The diarrhea rate last year was a whopping 56% !!

We have set up our basic motorcycle course in such a way that practically no driving lessons are required (provided you reach and practice the course goals). A test drive lesson gives a feeling of what it will be like during the test and serves as a location assessment.

Motorbike trial course

Motorcycling is not just a way to get around, it is passion and embodies a feeling of freedom.

In this course we show you the first steps and impart the necessary know-how and the ability to move a motorcycle.

With a lot of humor we combine theory with practice in such a way that you would like to keep going all day long.

Basic motorcycle courses

In categories A1 and A, you must complete the practical basic course within four months of the date of issue of the learning driving license. It is 8 hours for category A1 and 12 hours for category A. After that, the validity of the learning driving license is automatically extended by one year.

With a category A learning driving license, you may not complete the basic training with a category A1 vehicle.

Motorcycle curve training

Cornering is the most beautiful thing about motorcycling. With the right technology, safety, fun and joy increase. We practice that on this daily course. We learn to consciously implement the curves and to drive them on a safe line. The goal is to make curves as round and dynamic as possible.

With high-tech and know-how, we show you how beautiful cornering can be.

Motorcycle test requirements

Clothing

Category A1
  • Motorcycle helmet (tested according to ECE regulation 22)
  • Motorcycle gloves or gloves made from abrasion and tear-resistant material
  • Robust jacket and trousers made of abrasion and tear resistant material
  • Motorbike boots or ankle-protecting, sturdy shoes

The protective requirements for clothing must also be met when wearing rainwear.

Category A and A limited (35KW)
  • Motorcycle helmet (tested according to ECE regulation 22)
  • Motorcycle gloves
  • Motorcycle jacket
  • Motorcycle pants
  • Motorbike boots or ankle-protecting sturdy shoes

The protective requirements for clothing must also be met when wearing rainwear.

Motorcycle

Vehicle Cat. A limited (35kw)

A 2-wheeled, 2-seater motorcycle without a sidecar with an engine power of at most 35 kW and a ratio of engine power and unladen weight of not more than 0.20 kW / kg. No motorcycle in subcategory A1.

Vehicle Cat. A

A motorcycle without a sidecar with an engine output of at least 35 kW and two seats.

Vehicle category A1

A two-wheel motorcycle without a sidecar with a maximum displacement of 125 cm3 and an engine output of maximum 11 kW. (For handlebars 18 years at most 50 cm3). If a test vehicle with a maximum speed of 45 km / h is used, the category A1 is limited to 45 km / h.
